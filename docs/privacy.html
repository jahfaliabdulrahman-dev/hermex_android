Privacy Policy for Hermex Android

Last updated: July 16, 2026

Summary

Hermex Android does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. It is a client application that connects directly to your own Hermes Agent server. All data stays between your phone and your server.

What Data Does Hermex Access?

  • Server connection details (URL, API key) — stored locally on your device using encrypted storage (Flutter Secure Storage). Never transmitted to any third party.
  • Chat messages and sessions — retrieved from your Hermes Agent server and displayed in the app. Stored locally in an Isar database on your device for offline access.
  • Task/job configurations — retrieved from and sent to your Hermes Agent server.

What Data Does Hermex NOT Collect?

  • ✗ No analytics
  • ✗ No crash reporting
  • ✗ No advertising identifiers
  • ✗ No device fingerprinting
  • ✗ No location tracking
  • ✗ No personal information
  • ✗ No usage statistics

Third-Party Services

Hermex Android does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The only network connections made are to the Hermes Agent server you configure.

Data Storage

  • Connection credentials: stored in Android Keystore (hardware-backed encryption)
  • Chat history and sessions: stored in local Isar database on your device
  • Settings and preferences: stored in SharedPreferences on your device

All data remains on your device and your server. We have no access to it.

Your Control

  • You can delete all local data by clearing the app's storage in Android Settings
  • You can disconnect from any server at any time
  • You own your data — it lives on your server, not ours
